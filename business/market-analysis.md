# Virtue PWA Market Analysis

## Category
Productivity planning apps with neurodivergent-friendly workflow design.

## Tailwinds
- Strong demand for simpler planning systems.
- Rising interest in ADHD-specific tooling.
- Offline-first and ownership values in productivity tooling.

## Headwinds
- Extremely crowded task management market.
- AI planning features are becoming commodity.

## Competitor Archetypes
- Mainstream task apps.
- Kanban/project management suites.
- ADHD planner apps.
- AI to-do assistants.

## White Space
Low-friction Eisenhower workflow with offline-first reliability and optional AI, not AI-first complexity.

## Validation Data to Collect
- Onboarding completion and activation time.
- Week-2 retention by user segment.
- Premium collaboration feature demand.
