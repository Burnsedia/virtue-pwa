# Virtue PWA Business Plan

## Product Role
Virtue is an offline-first planning tool that helps users prioritize work with an Eisenhower matrix workflow.

## ICP
- Primary: solo builders and ADHD/neurodivergent planners.
- Secondary: small teams needing visual priority planning.

## FOSS and Monetization Boundary
- Free forever: offline personal planning core.
- Paid: collaboration, cloud sync, advanced templates, optional AI decomposition.

## Business Model
- Free local planner drives adoption.
- Paid team convenience and collaboration features drive revenue.

## Primary KPI
- Week-2 retention after first project creation.

## 90-Day Objectives
- Stabilize onboarding and first-project flow.
- Reach 30% week-2 retention for activated users.
- Build premium waitlist for collaboration features.
