# Virtue PWA Marketing Plan

## Positioning
Plan priorities clearly with an offline-first workflow built for real attention constraints.

## Core Channels
- GitHub: docs-first onboarding and roadmap transparency.
- X/Twitter: workflow demos, before/after planning examples.
- Niche communities: productivity and ADHD-focused groups.

## Content Cadence
- Weekly: one feature or workflow demo post.
- Biweekly: one template/use-case spotlight.
- Monthly: retention and roadmap update.

## Funnel and CTAs
- Primary CTA: Use offline open version.
- Secondary CTA: Join premium collaboration waitlist.
- Events: landing_view, first_project, first_matrix, week2_return, premium_waitlist.

## 90-Day Experiments
- Compare ADHD-first copy vs generic productivity copy.
- Test starter template packs for onboarding completion.
- Test short demo clips vs static screenshots for CTR.
